## ASSESSMENT REPORT T&Cs TEMPLATE

**Location in PDF**: After "ASSESSMENT NOTES" section, before footer

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
TERMS & CONDITIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

This assessment report has been compiled in accordance with the instructions 
received and represents our professional opinion based on the vehicle condition 
at the time of inspection.

INSPECTION SCOPE:
• Visual inspection only - no invasive testing performed
• Assessment based on vehicle condition at time of inspection
• Pre-incident and incident damages discussed with client
• Warranty and service history confirmed where available

ASSESSMENT VALIDITY:
• This assessment is valid for 30 days from the inspection date
• Vehicle condition may change - reassessment recommended if repairs delayed
• Assessment based on information available at time of inspection

LIABILITY & LIMITATIONS:
• Assessment is professional opinion only, not a guarantee
• Hidden or latent defects may not be visible during inspection
• Assessor not responsible for mechanical failures post-inspection
• Assessment does not cover future deterioration or wear

ADDITIONAL INSPECTIONS:
• Further inspections may be required if damage extent changes
• Additional costs may apply for reassessments or supplementary inspections
• Client to notify assessor immediately of any new damage discovered

CONFIDENTIALITY:
• This report is confidential and for authorized use only
• Report may not be reproduced or distributed without written consent
• Information contained herein is privileged and protected

For queries or concerns regarding this assessment, please contact:
[COMPANY_NAME] | [PHONE] | [EMAIL]
```

## ESTIMATE T&Cs TEMPLATE

**Location in PDF**: After "NOTES" section, before footer

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
TERMS & CONDITIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

THIS IS NOT AN AUTHORISATION FOR REPAIRS UNLESS ACCOMPANIED BY AN OFFICIAL 
AUTHORISATION FROM [COMPANY_NAME].

ESTIMATE VALIDITY:
• This estimate is valid for 30 days from the date of issue
• Prices subject to change after validity period
• Revalidation required if repairs not commenced within 30 days

SCOPE OF WORK:
• Estimate covers only items listed above
• Additional repairs not quoted for must be reported immediately
• No work to commence on unlisted items without written approval
• All additional requests must be logged via [PORTAL_URL]

LABOUR RATES:
• Labour Rate: R[RATE]/hour
• Paint Rate: R[RATE]/panel
• Rates subject to change with 30 days notice

PARTS & MATERIALS:
• OEM parts used unless alternative approved in writing
• Part availability subject to supplier stock
• Substitute parts may be used if originals unavailable (with approval)
• All parts carry manufacturer warranty

SPECIAL NOTES:
• ACRRR - AIRCON RECOVERY, RECYCLE AND RECHARGE
• NOT SPECIFIED - Item not covered under policy, will not be allowed
• Betterment applied to worn components (see notes for details)

ACCEPTANCE & COMMENCEMENT:
• Acceptance of this estimate constitutes acceptance of current SLA
• Commencement of repairs implies acceptance of these terms
• If not in agreement, DO NOT COMMENCE REPAIRS - contact office immediately

PRE-REPAIR CHECKS:
• Repairer must check for additional repairs not quoted for
• Repairer must check for engine and gearbox damage before commencing
• Any additional damage discovered must be reported immediately

INVOICE & PAYMENT:
• All invoices for payment must be sent to [PRINCIPLE/BROKER]
• [COMPANY_NAME] not liable for outstanding payments not paid by principle
• Payment terms: [STANDARD_TERMS]

DELAYS & PROBLEMS:
• Any delays or problems must be reported to office immediately
• Contact: [EMAIL] | [PHONE]
• Delays may affect claim settlement timeline

ESTIMATE CHANGES:
• NO CHANGES to repair estimate or authorized amount without written approval
• Any additional repairs/parts must be reported to office
• All additional requests must be logged online with photographs
• To prevent delays, all additional requests must include photos of damaged part

ADDITIONALS POLICY:
• NO ADDITIONALS allowed after final costing has been completed
• All additionals must be pre-approved before repair completion
• Unapproved additionals will not be paid

QUALITY & AUDIT:
• Principle/Broker reserves right to withhold payment if:
  - Repairs not carried out in satisfactory condition
  - Work-in-progress audit not afforded
  - Invoice audit not afforded

FINAL COSTING:
• Once repair work completed, final repair costing must be done by office
• All final costings must be sent to [FRC_EMAIL]
• Required documentation:
  - All parts invoices
  - All outwork invoices (air-con, alignment, diagnostics, etc.)
  - Collection/Release notice signed by insured
  - Copy of workmanship warranty/guarantee

For questions or concerns: [COMPANY_NAME] | [PHONE] | [EMAIL]
```

## FRC (FINAL REPAIR COSTING) T&Cs TEMPLATE

**Location in PDF**: After line items/decisions, before footer

```
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
FINAL REPAIR COSTING - TERMS & CONDITIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SCOPE OF FRC:
• Final costing based on actual parts and labour used
• Comparison against original estimate provided
• Additionals reviewed and approved/declined as per policy

DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS:
All of the following must be submitted for FRC completion:
✓ All parts invoices (with part numbers and quantities)
✓ All outwork invoices (air-con, wheel alignment, diagnostics, etc.)
✓ If work done in-house: printouts from relevant machines
✓ Completed Collection/Release Notice (signed by insured or agent)
✓ Copy of workmanship warranty/guarantee for completed work

ADDITIONALS POLICY:
• ONLY additionals approved by office PRIOR to repair completion allowed
• No additionals permitted after repair completion
• All pre-approved additionals must be documented with approval reference

VARIANCE TOLERANCE:
• Minor variances (±5%) acceptable without revalidation
• Variances exceeding 5% require written explanation
• Significant variances may require reassessment

PAYMENT TERMS:
• Payment due within [STANDARD_TERMS] of FRC approval
• Payment to [COMPANY_NAME] or as directed by principle
• Late payment subject to interest charges

QUALITY ASSURANCE:
• All work must meet industry standards
• Workmanship warranty minimum [PERIOD] from completion
• Defects must be reported within warranty period

DISPUTE RESOLUTION:
• Any disputes regarding FRC must be raised within 14 days
• Supporting documentation required for all disputes
• Disputes resolved through [DISPUTE_PROCESS]

SIGN-OFF:
• FRC completion requires engineer sign-off
• Sign-off confirms all work completed to specification
• Sign-off authorizes payment processing

For FRC submissions: [FRC_EMAIL]
For queries: [PHONE] | [EMAIL]
```

## NOTES SECTION INTEGRATION

**Current Display** (in Assessment Report):
```
ASSESSMENT NOTES
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Client requested a repairer in the Springs area.
Client was not present at the assessment. Interview was done telephonically.
Client explained that he was driving when he noticed rocks on the road...

NB: Betterment applied to the right front tyre due to wear and tear, 37.86%
and betterment applied to the left front tyre, 35.71% due to wear and tear.

Incident and pre incident damages were discussed with the client.
Seatbelts pre-tensioners have been inspected and have not deployed.
```

**Professional Format** (with proper data extraction):
```
NOTES
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
[All notes from assessment_notes table, concatenated with timestamps]

Each note displays:
- Note title (if exists)
- Note text
- Source tab (which assessment section note came from)
- Timestamp
```
